About Us

We warmly welcome you to Sri Puchong Hotel, where we strive to ensure that your stay is not only enjoyable but also exceptionally comfortable. Our commitment to enhancing your experience means that we are constantly looking for ways to serve you better.
Since opening our doors on November 1, 2006, Hotel Sri Puchong has established itself as a premier six-story destination in the heart of the bustling business center of Bandar Puteri, Puchong. With 93 well-appointed rooms, each featuring air conditioning, a telephone, a personal minibar, a mini refrigerator, LCD TV with selected Astro channels, and hot water shower facilities, we provide everything you need for a relaxing stay.
Our prime location offers easy access to Kuala Lumpur and KL International Airport (KLIA), just a 20- to 30-minute drive away. You’ll find major hypermarkets like Tesco and the vibrant IOI shopping malls nearby. Moreover, the Giant Shopping Centre, just a 10-minute walk from the hotel, includes a Post Office for your convenience. In Bandar Puteri, indulge your taste buds with a plethora of dining options, from steamboats and Korean cuisine to Japanese, Chinese, Malay, and Western dishes. Whether you're traveling for business, with family, or for leisure, our budget hotel provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable experience. Join us at Hotel Sri Puchong, where comfort and convenience meet excellence.

Our Location

Perfectly situated in the vibrant heart of Puchong's business, shopping, and entertainment district. With effortless access to Kuala Lumpur’s commercial hub, you’ll find yourself just 20 minutes away from the city’s dynamic attractions. Additionally, both KLIA and Putrajaya are a mere 30-minute drive from our doorstep.
